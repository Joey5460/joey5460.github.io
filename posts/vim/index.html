shortcut

delete

-dt/df 'c' --- not include c/include c - db - de - d$ - c w: delete word and insert mode - Ctrl + w: delelte previous word in insert mode - shift+c: delete till end and insert

move

  • A: move to end then edit

  • b --- word begining

  • e --- word ending

  • w --- next word

ctrl+n ctrl+p

set tags+=~/.vim/tags/qt5
